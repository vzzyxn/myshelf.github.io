Shoe dog

About

A memoir of Phil Knight the first man behind the revolutionary swoosh. The book records behind the scenes of the making of Nike also know as Blue Ribbon in the earlier stage of business.

That’s Nike

Blue Ribbon a small startup based on Oregon got to start a business with a Japanese shoe makers Onitsuka. Later there business became the most demanded one among the market in United States but due to the unacceptable late deliveries and cash flow problems both the compaines face a law time. However Nike surfed through the time and got a compensation but the sinister moments hadn’t end.
